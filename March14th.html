Where i've been

Hey there, if you know me then you've probably wondered where i've been as of late, given that i don't usually go more than a day without being online and as of now it's been around two weeks. Short answer: I'm doing ok, i'll slowly go back into talking to people again and there's no need to worry about me. If you want to talk i will try to respond, but please be patient as there's a lot on my mind.

Long answer

At the end of February i had a consultation with my endocrinologist about my progress on HRT, we talked about various things and went through some issues i've been having with integrating into society as a trans woman, such as worries about my voice, clothes, etc. Overall it went fairly well but at the end of it i raised concerns i've had about breast development, as it had been around 6 months since i felt i've had any notable growth, she gave me a at the physical checkup, she gave me the news that it was very likely that i'm done growing my breasts and they will not get any bigger, for a multitude of reasons this ended up giving me a severe emotional breakdown, to a degree i've never dealt with before, making me fully bedridden for 5 days where i didn't even have the energy to turn on my computer and make food, i would've been way worse if i didn't have my grandma who i live with to support me. Afterwards i got somewhat better but still didn't feel like talking to people, looking up things to prove or disprove my endo's assertion both didn't and did help, but overall i felt like something inside me died irrevocably, seeing a hope that i had been nurturing slowly through the years wilt away, the main reason i sought out HRT and the only real thing i felt i was missing. It also was a bit disheartening to have everyone around me just suggest to get implants, yes implants are great and i'm happy they exist but they were never something i wanted to pursue, somewhat due to the expense of course but mostly due to my weird mental aversions towards them, i wanted to be the all-natural amazoness that i fantasized about, to not even give shitty transphobes any ammunition to be horrible to me, that i looked how i did purely but my own dedication, the mere thought of horrible comments people could say to me is enough to not make me even consider it altogether. So yeah, basically i feel quite hopeless in that regard given that neither the all-natural nor augmented options work.

(Sidenote: No, you don't need breasts to be a woman, you don't need breasts to pass, ultimately it doesn't really matter and even then you can get implants which are also completely valid, i may not want them for myself but i'm NOT discrediting anyone who does, i think they're awesome, none of what i'm writing here applies to anyone else but me, so don't take it as advice, only as my personal experiences)

I've thought about it for a long time now, it has been devastating and a major hit to my mental health, something that i secretly felt all along but could easily brush aside with the simple thought of "give it time", if i knew that i'd have what i'd want in 1, 3, 5 years then i would not care at all, i'm patient, but when the ETA for what i want goes from Unknown to Never, it's hard not to spiral. It's not fully certain of course, she could be wrong, i could change my diet, it could change my meds, i could get implants even if i feel iffy about them and even if my size is set, they should still fill up laterally over the years and get fuller overall. I've not given up, i'm still a woman, i will still be here, i will still pursue a better life for myself, it's just became a bit harder as all, but i am a resilient person with people that can help me.
